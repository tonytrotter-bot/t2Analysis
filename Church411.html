Help & Tips

How to use Church411

Photos: Click any empty placeholder circle to upload. You can also upload from the photo editor or when adding a new member.

Adjust a photo: Long-press a member card, then use the Photo tab to reposition and zoom.

Search: The search bar finds people by name, nickname, church, or anything in their notes.

Nicknames: Add a nickname via the notes editor (pencil icon on card back). It shows on the main grid and cards.

Sync: All changes sync automatically across your devices via the cloud.

Tip: Square images look best in the circular frames.

Bookmarks: Long-press the ribbon icon on a card's notes side to bookmark or un-bookmark a member. Filter by "Bookmarked" in Member Type.

Family Cards: Members with "&" in their name (e.g. "John & Jane Smith") are treated as couples. Upload a second spouse photo in the photo editor — the coin will flip between photos when tapped. Tap the name or info area to see notes.
